NAICS code
A NAICS code corresponds to a category of industries. NAICS codes categorize all industries on various levels of specificity.
NAICS codes
NAICS codes are used to categorize all industries on a variety of levels of specificity. Our modules use them to initialize NAICS trees. We choose to use NAICS codes as our standard because they “are the standard used by Federal statistical agencies in classifying business establishments” (North American Industry Classification System), and nearly all our data sources are Federal statistical agencies.
NAICS tree
A data structure tree with industry objects as nodes.
